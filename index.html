Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Bei Yu since Fall 2017. Previously, I received Master of Science with Merit from Department of Computing, Imperial College London in 2016, and Master of Engineering from USTC in 2015.

My research interests include machine learning and deep learning with applications in VLSI CAD and image processing.
