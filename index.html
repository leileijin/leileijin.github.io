Biography

I'm a Ph.D. at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), with Prof. Tsung-Yi Ho since 2025. Previously, I got my Ph.D. from Southeast Univeristy (SEU) in 2024.

Research Interest

  • Crosstalk-aware Timing Analysis and optimization
  • Backside PDN PPA Optimization
  • Cross-layer Timing and Power Analysis
